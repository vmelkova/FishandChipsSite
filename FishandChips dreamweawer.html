Welcome to FairField Fish and Chips

FairField Fish & Chips has owned and operated family-run fish and chips restaurants for over 40 years. Now with 7 locations to serve you on Vancouver Island and the Vancouver Lower Mainland, the finest fish and chips are just a car ride away. And you will be glad you made the trip. Our fish and chips are made to order, from only the best halibut and seafood available on the market. Unlike other restaurants that serve a pre-breaded, frozen product, our customers can watch their fresh piece of fish be cut, cooked and served in front of them.
